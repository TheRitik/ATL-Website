SNEH International School successfully organized the Winter Camp - Minecraft Hackathon from January 9-14, 2025. The four-day event, themed "Survival Innovation," aimed to foster creativity, technology, and innovation among students. The event was inaugurated by esteemed guests, including Regional Mentor Ms. Viji Sridhar, Nodal Officer Ms. Neha Jain, Director Ms. Kamesh Jacob, and Director Strategic Planning Ms. Alka Mathur. The hackathon commenced with a motivational workshop by Er. Rajneesh Singh, Senior STEM Innovation Engineer, who guided students through advanced Minecraft concepts, redstone engineering, and collaborative teamwork. Subsequent days featured thrilling challenges, including Parkour, Mob Arena, and Base Design Building, which encouraged participants to think creatively, work together, and push their problem-solving skills. 9 teams participated, with 3 teams winning trophies and all participants receiving certificates. Renowned mentors and judges, including Ms. Viji Sridhar and Dr. Neha Jain, guided and evaluated the students. The event concluded with an insightful session, encouraging students to embrace innovation and explore technology.
